USP Ninjas - Timed Deathmatch
Mapping  by Nathaniel Miller AKA NIPPER
maps @ www.geocities.com/nipper14223

An apartment building has been taken 
over by terrorist-ninjas!  Each team 
must use their stealth-tactics and 
ninja-skills to defeat their enemy.

CTs:  Infiltrate the building and 
eliminate all terrorists before the 
round time is up.

Ts:  Hide in the shadows and try to 
ambush the CTs.
